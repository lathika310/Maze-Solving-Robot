This is the final project for the "COE538 Microprocessor Systems" 5th semester course for the
Bachelors in Computer Engineering Undergraduate Degree at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly known as Ryerson).

The project was done in a group of 3. The members were Hamza Hashmi, Ian Naunheimer and myself.

the "eebot", a maze solving guided robot.
